Otter Info

Otter Facts

The information found on this page was obtained from the otter Wikipedia page.


  • An otter's den is called a holt or clutch.
  • They are notable for their ability to use stones to break open shellfish on their stomachs. This skill must be learned by the young.
  • Prey-dependence leaves otters very vulnerable to prey depletion.
Odder Facts
  • Otters have been known to make and enjoy their own water slides.
Diet
